## Monthly Retainer Options

### Tier 1 — Maintenance Only ($0/mo)
- Hosting stays free (Cloudflare Pages)
- Client manages their own social media / content
- We fix critical bugs on request
- **Best for:** Client who is self-sufficient and just needed the site built

### Tier 2 — Lite Upkeep ($150–250/mo)
- Monthly content refresh (update services, pricing, seasonal cocktail menu)
- Performance & security checks
- Google Analytics or Cloudflare Analytics review
- 1 new section or feature per month (e.g., photo gallery, testimonials update)
- Social media link updates
- **Best for:** Client who wants a living site without managing it themselves

### Tier 3 — Growth Package ($350–500/mo)
Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- SEO optimization (meta tags, schema markup, Google Business Profile updates)
- Monthly blog or content piece (cocktail recipe post, event recap)
- Instagram/TikTok Reels embedded or spotlighted on site
- Email capture integration (Mailchimp or similar)
- Google Ads or Meta Ads landing page support
- Monthly analytics report with recommendations
- **Best for:** Client actively trying to grow bookings via digital presence

### Tier 4 — Full Digital Partner ($750–1,200/mo)
Everything in Tier 3, plus:
- Social media management (2–3 posts/week drafted and scheduled)
- Paid ad campaigns (Google Local Services, Instagram Boosts)
- Photo/video asset sourcing and optimization
- Event booking system integration (Calendly, HoneyBook, or custom)
- Review management (Google, Yelp, The Knot)
- Monthly strategy call
- **Best for:** Client ready to scale bookings and dominate NYC bartending market

## Recommended Starting Point

**Start with Tier 2 ($200/mo)** — keeps the site fresh and professional while they evaluate ROI. After 3 months, upsell to Tier 3 if they're seeing leads come in from the site.
